Complaints Process
We want to give you the best possible service. However, if at any point you become unhappy or concerned about the service we provided then you should inform us immediately, so that we can do our best to resolve the problem.
In the first instance please contact the person who is working on your case to discuss your concerns and we will do our best to resolve any issues at this stage. If you would like to make a formal complaint, please telephone or write to us detailing your specific concerns.
We undertake to investigate any complaint objectively and promptly so as to achieve an early resolution. It is our policy to investigate and hopefully resolve your complaint within eight weeks of your written notice. Making a complaint will not affect how we handle your case.
What do to if we cannot resolve your complaint:
The IAA (our regulator) can help you if we are unable to resolve your complaint ourselves. They will look at your complaint independently and it will not affect how we handle your case.

Accessibility Policy
Introduction
1stCallUK Immigration Services is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.
Our Commitment
We aim to make our website and services accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.
Standards and Guidelines
Our website strives to conform to the following standards and guidelines:
• Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1: We aim to meet or exceed the Level AA criteria.
• Accessible Rich Internet Applications (ARIA): We utilize ARIA principles to enhance the accessibility of web content.
Measures to Support Accessibility
1stCallUK Immigration Services takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:
• Incorporate accessibility into our web development process: Accessibility is considered from the earliest stages of design through development and testing.
• Provide ongoing training for our staff: Our team receives training on accessibility best practices.
• Regularly test our website: We perform regular accessibility audits and user testing to identify and fix issues.
Feedback
We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:
• Email: info@1stcalluk.com (mailto:info@1stcalluk.com)
• Phone: +44 1234 567890
We try to respond to feedback within 5 business days.
Accessibility Features
Our website includes several features designed to improve accessibility:
• Alternative Text for Images: We provide alternative text for all images to ensure that they are accessible to users who rely on screen readers.
• Keyboard Navigation: Our website can be navigated using a keyboard, ensuring accessibility for users who cannot use a mouse.
• Text Resizing: Users can resize text to their comfort level using browser settings.
• Contrast and Color: We ensure sufficient contrast between text and background, and avoid using color alone to convey information.
Limitations and Alternatives
Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations and potential solutions:
• Third-Party Content: Some third-party content and features may not be fully accessible. We do not control these vendors, but we encourage them to provide content that is accessible and provide alternatives where possible.
Ongoing Efforts
We are committed to ongoing improvement. Our accessibility efforts are continuous as we strive to meet the evolving needs of our users.
